Abandoned Calls

An abandoned call is a call placed to a PSAP in which the caller disconnects before the call can be answered.

1. The Calltaker shall call back the caller utilizing the Automatic Number Identification furnished with the call.

2. The Calltaker shall solicit sufficient information to determine what type of response is necessary.

3. If the call back results in no answer, a police response shall be initiated for an incomplete call.
