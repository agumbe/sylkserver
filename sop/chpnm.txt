The following locations/conditions will always have law enforcement dispatched on medical calls:

* Bars

* Known trouble spots

* Known large crowds

* Suspected crime involved

* Any time the dispatcher feels there is a need

* When requested by EMS unit at scene



Initial Dispatch will include the following information:

* Units to respond

* Location

* Chief Complaint

* Case Entry Information

* Other Pertinent Information from the Call

* MPDS Determinant Code
----
Is the person breathing?

Is the person Conscious?

Does the person have a history of heart problems?
